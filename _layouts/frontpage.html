The Bible: It's all about Jesus...

In true Christianity, people are set free from the perpetual bondage of the treadmill of performance to meet their perceived standard of spiritual perfection. This treadmill is the foundation of virtually all of the world's religions. God gives us through Christ a way to jump off this treadmill. Whatever our spiritual needs are, God gives us also in Christ. Need of forgiveness? Need of rest? Need of burden lifted? Need of meaning? Need of everlasting life? All found in Christ. It is written in Romans 8:32: "Indeed, he who did not spare his own Son, but gave him up for us all--how will he not also, along with him, freely give us all things?"
